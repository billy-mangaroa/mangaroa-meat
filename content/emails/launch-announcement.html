Fresh beef and lamb from Mangaroa Farms

Today is the day. Fresh, locally processed beef and lamb are now available at the Mangaroa Farms shop and online for pickup.

Our animals are grown in harmony with nature on regenerative pastures right here in Mangaroa Valley. After professional slaughter, the carcasses come home and Rob and the crew handle all the butchery and processing. The same people who raise the animals prepare the cuts. Shorter chain, more care, fresher meat.

What is available

Standard retail cuts of beef and lamb — steaks, mince, sausages, chops, roasts, and more. The range changes week to week.
