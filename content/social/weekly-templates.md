# Weekly Social Rhythm — Post-Launch Templates

Establish a repeatable weekly cadence. Adjust which days work best for your audience.

---

## Monday — This Week's Cuts
**Format:** Photo of this week's product + simple list.

**Template caption:**
This week from the butchery: [LIST CUTS]. Fresh in the shop now, or order online for pickup at meat.mangaroa.org.

**Image:** Cabinet photo or product flat-lay on butcher paper.

---

## Wednesday — Recipe / Cooking Tip
**Format:** Simple cooking idea using one of the available cuts.

**Template caption:**
[CUT NAME] — [SIMPLE COOKING METHOD]. [1-2 sentences about how to prepare it]. Pick one up from the shop or order online.

**Examples:**
- "Lamb shoulder — low and slow at 150C for 3-4 hours with rosemary and garlic. The house smells incredible and the meat pulls apart. Pick one up from the shop."
- "Beef mince — the base of everything. Bolognese, nachos, cottage pie. Ours is pasture-raised, home-processed, and fresh this week."
- "Scotch fillet — keep it simple. Salt, pepper, hot pan, 3 minutes a side. Let it rest. Done."

---

## Friday — Farm Story / Behind the Scenes
**Format:** Photo or short video from the farm or butchery.

**Template caption:**
[STORY ANGLE]. [1-2 sentences about what is happening on the farm this week]. Drop in this weekend — the shop is open [HOURS].

**Story angles to rotate through:**
- Animals on pasture (seasonal: winter feed, spring lambs, Holter cattle grazing)
- Rob and the crew at work in the butchery
- Farm landscape / seasonal changes
- Virtual fencing in action — how we manage grazing without physical fences
- Cover crops and soil health — what regenerative looks like up close
- TB testing, cattle movement — real farm operations behind the scenes
- The supply chain: paddock to butchery to counter, all within the valley
- Filming days: capture raw footage during cattle movement, processing, farm walks

---
