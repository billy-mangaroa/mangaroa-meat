## Voice & structure notes (for Grady's review)

- **Load-bearing phrase across all posts:** "Locally raised. Locally processed." — three beats, period-spaced, used as both differentiation and rhythm. Same phrase as the email subject.
- **No banned openers** (no "What a [adjective]", no "There's nothing quite like", no "We're so excited", no "It was such a"). All posts open with a stated fact or a seasonal frame.
- **No banned adjectives** anywhere (beautiful / wonderful / amazing / fantastic / magical / incredible / gorgeous / stunning / inspiring / heartwarming).
- **Emoji count:** IG/FB max 2 per caption. Bluesky max 1 per thread. Stories can use stickers freely.
- **Te reo natural, not translated:** hōtoke, mauri, paddock-to-plate. The reader either knows or feels it.
- **Rob is named.** No surnames. Roles, not portraits — the photo brief explicitly avoids the "team-member-as-marketing-asset" pattern flagged in the brand voice.
- **One CTA per post** (online OR in-shop, not both shouted). The email is the place that pairs them; social is one beat at a time.
- **Address:** 98 Whitemans Valley Road, Upper Hutt (corrected — earlier draft had 108).
- **Length:** Caption A ~95 words, B ~50 words, day-2 ~45 words. Bluesky posts hand-trimmed to fit the 300-char limit comfortably with room for line breaks.
- **Hashtag stack:** 15 on launch day for reach, drop to 5 on day-2 for register match. No platform-shouting hashtags (#viral / #explorepage / etc.).
