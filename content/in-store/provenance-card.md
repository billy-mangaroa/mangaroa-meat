## Home-grown, home-processed.

Our beef and lamb are grown in harmony with nature on Mangaroa's regenerative pastures — open paddocks, healthy soil, clean water, and plenty of space.

After professional slaughter, the carcasses come home to Rob and the crew. They handle all the butchery and processing right here in the valley. The same people who raise the animals prepare the cuts.

Shorter chain. More care. Fresher meat.

Every cut in this cabinet can be traced back to these paddocks and the people who work this land.
